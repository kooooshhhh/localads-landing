🧠 What is LocalAds?

LocalAds is an AI-powered ad generator that transforms a single product URL into a complete ad strategy and multiple ready-to-use creatives. Instead of manually creating ads, the platform automates the entire process.

In one line: LocalAds turns your product page into a test-ready ads engine.
