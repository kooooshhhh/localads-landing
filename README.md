## 🧠 What is LocalAds?

**LocalAds** is an AI-powered tool that helps D2C brands and marketers generate complete ad campaigns directly from a product URL.

Instead of spending hours brainstorming, writing, and designing ads  LocalAds automates the entire process.

---

## ⚙️ How it Works

1. Paste your product URL  
2. AI analyzes your product, positioning, and audience  
3. Get a complete ad strategy + creatives instantly  

---

## What You Get

- 🎯 Ad strategy (angles, messaging, positioning)
- ✍️ High-converting ad copy (headlines + descriptions)
- 🎬 Creative ideas (hooks, visuals, formats)
- 🧪 Multiple variations for testing
- 📊 Audience targeting suggestions
